A virtual world that encourages kids to explore and learn

Learning Landscapes is a place where kids can learn any kind of subject in schools. We are trying to get kids to enjoy learning while playing games co-op with peers and teachers.

What is our goal at Learning Landscapes?

Our goal, simply is to create a place where kids can have fun and learn required curriculum for their in class tasks. We are looking for a way to show teachers that learning through games is as easy as 1, 2, 3 and that their students will show that they learned the material because of the game.

R.I.T.
